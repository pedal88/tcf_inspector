What is TCF?

The Transparency and Consent Framework (TCF) is a digital advertising industry standard created by IAB Europe. It enables websites and apps to inform users about how their personal data is being processed and obtain their consent for various data processing purposes.

Key Components:

  • Vendors: Companies that process user data for advertising and measurement purposes
  • Purposes: Different ways that vendors can process user data (e.g., personalized ads, content measurement)
  • Legal Basis: The legal grounds under GDPR for processing user data (consent or legitimate interest)
